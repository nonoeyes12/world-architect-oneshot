# The Ethereal

A fully original one-shot generated by the World Architect framework: **Glassmere**, a stilt-city on
a black lake that powers its lamps with vesperlight drawn from its own dead — and the night a
drowned child's lantern comes home still lit. Eerie, melancholy, and built around a single
unbearable choice: may a town consume its dead to keep its children alive?

- `the-ethereal.md` — the campaign source (Markdown).
- `the-ethereal.pdf` — rendered with `tools/render.py` (maroon theme).
