# The Junktown Crusade

A genre-mashup one-shot demonstrating the framework across very different settings: a company of
crusader knights is flung 700 years forward by a dying pre-War machine and lands in a
post-apocalyptic scrap town on the brink of a gang war. Faith meets the atom; the "holy relic" they
carry turns out to be the machine's missing heart.

- `the-junktown-crusade.md` — the campaign source (Markdown).
- `the-junktown-crusade.pdf` — rendered with `tools/render.py` (rust theme).
